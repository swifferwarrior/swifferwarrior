About Me

Currently, I’m a therapist with an M.A. in Clinical Mental Health Counseling with a focus in neuro-based interventions. I provide child therapy, couple’s therapy, and individual and group A&D therapy. I’ve also worked extensively in crisis response, inpatient mental health, and in-home health for people with developmental and intellectual disabilities.

So why programming? Originally, it was out of frustration with poorly designed Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems. Mostly barebones web-portals without much consideration of what the end user needs. I set out to research what it would take to change that; if you can’t find a solution why not create one? Something that can auto-populate demographic fields in forms, provided tools for client management, form management, and was compliant with HIPAA standards.

I’m still taking incremental steps to accomplish this but found along the way that I really enjoy the challenge and constant learning involved with programming. More...
